Ready-to-Use Speaker Introduction

The Power of Purpose

Turning Opportunities into Impact

Good [morning/afternoon/evening], ladies and gentlemen.

It is my distinct privilege to introduce today’s speaker, Obiajuru Triumph Nwadiokwu, affectionately known as Obi.

Obi is a software engineer at an AI company, but his story reaches far beyond technology. It is a story of purpose, perseverance, scholarship, and service. He earned his bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems, graduating as valedictorian with a perfect academic record. He later earned a master’s degree in Information Systems Management from Carnegie Mellon University—one of the world’s leading institutions in computer science, artificial intelligence, robotics, data analytics, and information systems—and he is now pursuing a Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence.

His accomplishments are as inspiring as they are remarkable. Obi came to the United States on a fully funded scholarship and has earned more than $75,000 in scholarships throughout his academic journey. He is a White House HBCU Scholar, a Stanford University Innovation Fellow, a recipient of several innovation competition awards, and a published researcher in immunology and environmental biology.

Yet what truly distinguishes Obi is not simply what he has achieved, but what he chooses to do with every opportunity. He believes accomplishment reaches its highest purpose when it opens doors for others, improves lives, and strengthens communities.

Beyond his professional work, Obi enjoys traveling, exploring historical museums and archives, discovering new restaurants, and—if rice and Ayamase are on the menu—settling in for his favorite meal.

Today, Obi will present “The Power of Purpose: Turning Opportunities into Impact.” He will explore how a clear sense of purpose can transform obstacles into opportunities, inspire excellence, and empower ordinary people to create extraordinary change in their communities and beyond.

Ladies and gentlemen, please join me in giving a warm TED welcome to Obiajuru Triumph Nwadiokwu—Obi!
