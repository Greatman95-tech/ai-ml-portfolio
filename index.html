Artifact 1

PathWise AI Assistant: An AI/ML Career Readiness Chatbot

Introduction

PathWise AI Assistant is a custom AI chatbot created as part of my AIML-500 AI Lab. The assistant is designed to help graduate students studying artificial intelligence, machine learning, data analytics, and technology connect their coursework to real-world career readiness.

Artifact Link

Launch PathWise AI Assistant

Description

PathWise AI Assistant supports users with skill-gap identification, AI/ML concept explanation, project planning, resume preparation, GitHub portfolio guidance, and interview practice. The assistant was developed through a process that included testing regular LLM prompts, exploring a custom GPT, using STORM AI as a research assistant, and building a custom chatbot prototype.

Objective

The objective of this artifact was to create a practical AI assistant that helps graduate students move from classroom learning to career readiness. The assistant was designed to give structured guidance, ask clarifying questions, avoid dishonest resume writing, and help users identify clear next steps.

Process

I began by testing a regular LLM with prompts related to AI/ML career preparation. I then compared multiple responses to evaluate clarity, usefulness, specificity, realism, and organization. Next, I explored a custom GPT to understand how specialized AI assistants can provide more focused guidance. I also used STORM AI as a research assistant to study how AI assistants can support graduate students preparing for AI, data analytics, and machine learning careers.

After completing these steps, I used design thinking to plan and build PathWise AI Assistant. I considered the needs of graduate AI/ML students, defined the problem, brainstormed possible assistant features, created the custom GPT, and tested it with prompts related to study planning, resume bullets, and portfolio project recommendations.

Value Proposition

This artifact is valuable because it demonstrates my ability to use AI tools to design a practical solution for a specific audience. It shows that I can move beyond simply using AI for answers and instead use AI to build a structured, useful, and ethical assistant.

Unique Value

The unique value of PathWise AI Assistant is that it combines AI/ML learning support with career readiness. It does not only explain technical concepts; it also helps users think about projects, resumes, interviews, GitHub documentation, and professional development.

Relevance

This artifact is relevant to AI, machine learning, data analytics, education, and career development. It demonstrates skills that are important in the AI/ML field, including prompt engineering, user-centered design, ethical AI use, research synthesis, testing, and communication.
